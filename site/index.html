PHILOSOPHY

Technical Artist
Pushing Hardware, Pursuing Beauty

I am Cain Moriarty — a 3D technical artist specializing in photorealistic rendering and impossible physics. Working primarily in Blender with Cycles renderer, I create celestial forms, gravitational phenomena, and atmospheric studies where computational power meets artistic vision.

Every project pushes hardware limits in pursuit of visual truth. From multi-layer subsurface scattering to physics-accurate gravitational lensing, my work lives at the intersection of technical mastery and aesthetic ambition. Beauty earned through precision, impact achieved through discipline.
