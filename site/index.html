I am Cain Moriarty — a multidisciplinary creative working across 3D art and graphic design. In 3D, I build celestial forms and cosmic phenomena in Blender, pushing hardware limits for photorealistic renders. In graphic design, I create bold campaigns that challenge expectations and demand attention.

Whether crafting gravitational lensing through procedural nodes or designing guerrilla marketing that takes over city streets, the principle stays the same: technical mastery in service of fearless creativity. Beauty earned through discipline, impact achieved through craft.
